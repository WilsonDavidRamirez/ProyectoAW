StocX: Past, Present, Future


Josh Luber engaged Dan Gilbert, Greg Schwarz and Chris Kaufman in 2015 and launched StockX in February of 2016. The company currently has more than 800 employees at its Detroit headquarters, as of October 2018 opens its first international headquarters in London after moving to eBay in total transactions of sneakers in the previous year. In fact, the site has more than 10,000 transactions and a turnover of almost 2 million dollars per day. Impressive numbers, which give us an idea of how exponentially the phenomenon of resale in the last three years and how much is growing continuously. However, for Josh Luber is a natural evolution: "StockX was the natural and logical evolution of Campless," he told us, "The guide of prices of the shoes that I created in 2012/13. Over time I understood the true value of a pair of sneakers and knowing it I managed to create a portfolio of sneakers, and understanding how to structure a portfolio from there, you can create a real and proper stock market. StockX was born that way."
